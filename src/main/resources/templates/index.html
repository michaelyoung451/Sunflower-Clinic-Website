About Us

At Sunflower Clinic, we know that every client has different goals for their massage experience. We are dedicated to understanding your personal needs in order to work with you to reach those goals. Massage treatments have a host of benefits, such as reducing stress and anxiety, improving sleep and circulation, and relaxing muscles; but no matter what your reason for seeking a massage, we’re here to give you the very best treatment.
